Privacy Policy for Slop-ify Chrome Extension

Last Updated: September 22, 2025

Data Collection

Slop-ify does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely on your local device.

Local Storage

The extension only stores:

  • Your enable/disable preference
  • Word replacement count (stored locally on your device)

This data never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • linkedin.com access: To replace text on LinkedIn pages only
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options
  • scripting: To analyze and replace text content
  • activeTab: To access the current LinkedIn tab

Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The extension does not connect to external services or APIs.
